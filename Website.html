Welcome to Pippa's Tapsilogan, a warm and inviting family-owned restaurant that captures the essence of homemade Filipino cuisine. Situated in the heart of our community, we take pride in serving delicious and authentic dishes passed down through generations. At Pippa's Tapsilogan, we believe that food not only nourishes the body but also brings people together. Our carefully curated menu showcases a delightful blend of traditional flavors and contemporary twists, catering to both the adventurous and the comfort-seeking palates. From sizzling tapsilog to mouthwatering siomai, our talented chefs craft each dish with love and attention to detail. Whether you're craving the savory goodness of crispy porksilog or the comforting warmth of Lomi, our menu offers something for everyone. Step into our cozy, family-friendly establishment, where you'll be greeted by our friendly staff and a welcoming atmosphere. Our rustic yet modern decor creates the perfect backdrop for memorable meals and cherished conversations. Whether you're dining with loved ones or joining us for a quick bite during your lunch break, Pippa's Tapsilogan promises an experience that feels like home.
Welcome to Pippa's Sari-Sari Store, your one-stop destination for a delightful array of Filipino essentials and everyday treasures. As a beloved neighborhood sari-sari store, we take pride in providing convenience, charm, and a touch of nostalgia to our community. At Pippa's Sari-Sari Store, we understand the value of the little things that make life easier and more enjoyable. Step inside our welcoming store, filled to the brim with carefully curated products, from snacks and beverages to household necessities and delightful surprises. We've handpicked each item, ensuring quality, affordability, and an authentic taste of Filipino culture. Explore our shelves and discover a treasure trove of familiar favorites and hidden gems. From vibrant packets of traditional delicacies to refreshing beverages and savory snacks, we offer a wide selection that satisfies cravings and sparks fond memories. Looking for everyday essentials? We've got you covered with a range of toiletries, household items, and pantry staples, making us your go-to destination for all your needs.
